Methodology

Contract value = (revenue from withdrawals) − (cost of injections) − (storage cost × months held) − (injection/withdrawal handling fees) − (other per-event costs). Events are processed chronologically; injections fill storage up to capacity, withdrawals draw down available inventory. Any event that breaches the rate limit, storage capacity, or available inventory is flagged as invalid rather than silently priced.
